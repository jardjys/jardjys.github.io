Benchmark
The following information has the purpose of comparing ourselves with one of our competitors: HealthyKids(r) HealthyKids intends to do an app for children to be more healthy, involving parents and kids to work together.
Advantages over our Competitors:
Page 100% aimed for children. Children can understand for themselves nutritional information. Children will have full awareness of what they are eating.
Disadvatages over our Competitors:
Full initiative must come from the kids. Parents not fully involved.
